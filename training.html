Field training at Khayber Community College

Benefit gained from field training:

1- Field training represent the first experience for the student by applying what he has learnt in a real environment.
2- Provides an opportunity to interact and collaborate with experienced employees in the field.
3- Field training is an effective way to help the student acquire new capabilities and skills that he did not possess. These skills enable the student to expand his concepts, modify his directions, and consolidate his ability to innovate, create, and renew. He links the (theoretical) side of the acquired knowledge with the practical (work) product.
